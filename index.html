CMSC 388X: Introduction to Programming Languages Research for Undergraduates

Spring 2022

Lectures: Monday + Wednesday, 12:00pm–12:50pm, CSI 3117

Facilitators: Pierce Darragh, Justin Frank

Advised by: Leonidas Lampropoulos

Credits: 2

CMSC 388X is an introduction to research in the field of programming languages, specifically geared towards an undergraduate audience. We assume students will have taken CMSC 330 (Programming Languages) prior to this course.
